About Us

Welcome to FCI Assiut

The mission of the Faculty of Computers and information at Assiut University is to serve the students as well as the community through the provision of distinctive education and innovative research in the fields of computer science, information systems and technology, and multimedia. This faculty develops the students’ personalities to make them able to build a knowledge-based economy and to create an innovative, competitive, and self-learning work environment. Computers and Information graduates are able to compete locally, regionally, and internationally in the labor market and to interact creatively with society and entrepreneurs.

What will you learn?

SQL Mastery

Learn SQL from experienced data professionals. From basic queries to complex joins and subqueries, we’ll help you become confident in using SQL to extract, analyze, and manipulate data like a pro.

Excel for Data Analysis

Master Microsoft Excel, one of the most powerful and widely used tools in data analysis. Learn formulas, pivot tables, data cleaning, charts, and dashboards to analyze and present data effectively.

Power BI & Data Visualization

Create stunning dashboards and visual reports using Power BI. You'll learn how to connect data sources, build dynamic visuals, and gain insights from your data — a critical skill for any data analyst.
